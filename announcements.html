DCON 2020 Cancelled

Members of CNH Circle K:

Given the latest developments of the COVID-19 Virus Outbreak, and the significant and widespread cancellations by many colleges/universities, the 2020 California-Nevada-Hawaii Circle K District Convention has been CANCELLED. This has been a difficult decision that will impact students and adults across the three states, where the majority are in communities with no known active virus activity.

However, in the best interests and safety for all of our members and our communities, we have made the decision to cancel the convention this year.

Any potential refunds will be determined through CNH District negotiations with the Riverside Marriott and Riverside Convention Center.
